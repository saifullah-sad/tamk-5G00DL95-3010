# Answer to exercise "7.7 Tar 5"
#
tar -xjf ~/package.tar.bz2 -C ~ tmp/dir1/1.lst
Explanation: "-x" is extract, "-j" is decompress bzip2, "-f" is archive file, "-C ~" is to extract relative to home
and this only extracts "tmp/dir1/1.lst" without extracting the entire archive.
